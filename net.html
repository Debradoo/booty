About-us

Welcome to Ms. Me Foods! We are a company dedicated to providing high-quality tomato paste (Toma -puree) in variety of flavors to our customers. Our business was founded with a mission to offer a convenient, affordable and healthy solution for those who love the taste of fresh tomatoes but do not have the time or simply want to eliminate the stress associated with preparing a tomato paste.

We source our tomatoes from carefully selected farms and use only the freshest and ripest tomatoes to make our paste, while garnishing it with paprika, pepper, onion, chilies, garlic and ginger to produce a blend of different flavors. Our production is done under very hygienic conditions to ensure that the tomatoes are handled with care and processed quickly to preserve their nutritional value and natural flavor.

Toma-puree is made with a simple recipe of fresh organic tomatoes with all natural spices and flavor making it a healthy and wholesome addition to any recipe. We offer Toma-puree in a range of packaging options and variety of flavors to suit the needs of our customers.

We are committed to providing excellent customer service and ensuring that our products are of the highest quality. We pride ourselves on our transparency, and we are always happy to answer any questions our customers may have about our products.

Thank you for considering our tomato paste for your cooking needs. We look forward to serving you and providing you with the best possible product and service.
